I was born in a small town in Czech Republic. It didn't occur to me that I might chose to move abroad or even travel abroad much until I was 17. This simply wasn't what people around me did. Since the Czech Republic was part of the socialist block, my parents generation weren't allowed to travel quite so freely.

Yet, I ended up moving to London when I was 19.

What enabled me to be able to do so was that I got a job in IT when I was 18 and I started to make a decent sum of money. I moved to Prague and I ended up dropping out of my secondary school. I was never a fan of the formal education system (and, to the displease of my teachers, was pretty vocal about it).
