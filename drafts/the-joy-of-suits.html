title: The joy of suits tags: [suits] ---
I love wearing suits. These days I basically don't wear anything else. Unfortunately suits got bad rep these days. They've been accused of being "uncomfortable", "too corporate" and "stuffy". To me nothing could be more far from the truth.

A suit is man's purse

I don't like wearing bags. I never did, but before I started to wear suits, I simply had to. There is only so much you can fit into tiny jeans pockets.

With a suit, there is space for everything. Documents, map, money, debit card, hankerchief, pen, pocket knife, keys ... I sometimes even put my iPad mini into my pocket and no one could tell from the outside.

I like to get a fob pocket fitted into my trousers. With a fob pocket, it's simply impossible to lose keys, ever.

The "too-stuffy" myth

Really

Wool keeps you warm when it's cold and keeps you cool when it's hot. I always thought this is a bullshit.

The look

I'm a rather skinny guy. A good suit makes me look more muscular. Save your gym membership. What's not to like about that?
