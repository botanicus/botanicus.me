title: Mindful internet usage tags: [mindfulness] ---

Only recently I realised how bad internet addiction is. Having a device connected to the internet at your fingertips is like having a bottle of vodka by your bed.

I open my laptop and the WiFi is disabled. When I try to turn it on, it switches itself off in a few seconds.

That is unless I'm within hours I allowed myself to be online (between 9:30 and 18:20) and unless I specifically say "I'm going online for X minutes to do task Y".

And why on Earth am I doing that? Well, did it every happen to you that you woke up, went on Facebook, chatted with some friends, read your newsfeed, read some articles, watched a few videos and whops, suddenly it was time for dinner?

Yep. So that's why.
